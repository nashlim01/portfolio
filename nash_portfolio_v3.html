About me

Hi, I'm Nash — a data scientist who genuinely enjoys staring at model outputs at odd hours and thinking "wait, why is this working?" I'm from Sarawak, Malaysia, currently at Tencent Malaysia keeping a fleet of production ML models alive and useful.

My career so far has involved a lot of telling spreadsheets to behave themselves, convincing pipelines to stop breaking at 2am, and occasionally impressing stakeholders with a chart that somehow told a coherent story. My proudest achievement? A ~15% lift in identifying high-value users. My most humbling? The number of times I've run a SQL query and gotten exactly zero rows back.

If you're looking for someone who takes the work seriously but not themselves — you've found the right person.
